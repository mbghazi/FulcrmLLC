About Us

 

We are Fulcrm LLC, changing the paradigm of the ice industry in the Houston Metro Area.

 

The Pioneers of Quality Ice Vending in Houston Metro Area

In the bustling environment of Houston, where the sun casts its warm, sultry rays almost all year round, ice has become more than a luxury. It's a daily necessity. Yet, for the consumers, the struggle to get quality ice in a hassle-free manner remains real. That’s where Fulcrm LLC steps in. Addressing the Ice Paradox For years, the residents of Houston have had to make the grudging trip to their local stores or gas stations just to get their hands on a bag of ice. The challenge? Not only did they have to navigate through the crowded aisles, but the quality of the ice remained questionable at best. Most providers use water that's been barely filtered, leading to ice that might be contaminated. The Fulcrm LLC Advantage Enter the Everest VX4 ice and water machines – a revolutionary approach to delivering quality ice. These machines are not just any regular ice vending machines. They are designed to sanitize water meticulously with the help of 10-micron filters, ensuring that every particle, no matter how minute, is sieved out. Coupled with UV light treatment, the Everest VX4 ensures the production of pure, uncontaminated ice. The added layer of weatherization protection ensures these machines can stand up to the challenging Houston weather, guaranteeing consistent service. Now, you may wonder about the costs. Thanks to our unique business model that minimizes operational costs due to a smaller footprint compared to traditional stores and gas stations, Fulcrm LLC can offer a 10lb bag of pristine quality ice for as little as 2 dollars. Value, convenience, and quality rolled into one.

 

Convenience at its best

But that's not where the advantages end. One of the major pain points for consumers has been the restricted timings of stores. Fulcrm LLC removes this obstacle by providing access to quality ice 24/7. Whether it's a sunny afternoon or a silent night, residents of the Houston metro area can now get their ice without stepping inside a store.

 

A Revolution in the Ice Industry

In essence, Fulcrm LLC is not just a business; it's a movement. A movement to redefine the way Houston perceives ice. A movement to provide quality, convenience, and cost-efficiency. We're not just selling ice; we're changing the paradigm. Join us on this exciting journey as we continue to revolutionize the ice vending industry in the Houston Metro Area, one bag at a time.

 

Ownership & Structure

As the sole owner of Fulcrm LLC, Muhammad Ghazi retains full control and authority over all business decisions. As a startup, Fulcrm LLC has adopted a simple business structure. Mr. Ghazi oversees all daily operations and strategic planning. Major decisions regarding company vision and growth are made directly by him as the key stakeholder. This straightforward ownership and organizational model allows for nimble decision making and the flexibility to pivot as needed based on customer feedback and market changes. However, as the business expands, Mr. Ghazi will evaluate opportunities to bring on additional partners and investors to facilitate more rapid growth. Any changes to the ownership or structure would be made to support scale and long-term sustainability while still preserving the original vision of Fulcrm LLC.
